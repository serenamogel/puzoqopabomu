Is Luke Combs a liberal?

August 2024 · 2 minute read

SOME people have determined that country star Luke Combs is a Trump supporter, but that is not necessarily the case.

Many believe he does not support the Republican party due to him being vocal about the Black Lives Matter movement.

Is Luke Combs a supporter of Trump?

Luke Combs’ political affiliation is unknown. 

Combs has taken indirect political stances about his views such as choosing not to play at Trump’s famous Fourth of July White House concert in 2020.

Instead, he opted to play at PBS’ annual A Capitol Fourth event, held at the same time as the White House gathering.

He also shared on Instagram during #BlackoutTuesday on June 2, 2020, that he was “listening, learning, and understanding.”

In contrast, Trump denounced the Black Lives Matter movement and his supporters have raged against those who back the organization. 

Is Luke Combs married?

The country star married his fiancée Nicole Hocking during an intimate ceremony in Florida back in August 2020.

Speaking on Couple Things with Shawn + Andrew in May, they explained that their friendship turned into a relationship when they found themselves both single at 2016's Key West Songwriter's Festival.

He said: "I didn't have anything going on when we met. I didn't have a record deal, or a publishing deal, anything."

As Luke attempted to get his career off the ground, Nicole worked seven days a week between two jobs. 

When the time was right, she joined him on the road because Luke "would miss her not being there" on tour. 

The couple got engaged in fall 2018 when Luke got down on one knee in the kitchen of their home.
