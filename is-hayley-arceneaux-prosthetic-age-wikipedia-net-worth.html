Hayley Arceneaux will reach space with her prosthetic leg. We are very excited about this historic event.

St Jude Children’s Research Hospital announced that the hospital employee, Hayley Arceneaux, will be traveling to space.

She was selected as one of the four crew to board Inspiration4 on 15th September 2021.

Jared Isaacman, a billionaire, will be joining her on SpaceX’s first private spaceflight. He is the one paying for this entire mission.

Is Hayley Arceneaux Prosthetic?

Hayley Arceneaux had her leg replaced with a prosthetic one. She is also osteosarcoma, a type of bone cancer survivor.

Apparently, the pain started from her knee, which felt like a sprain.

She was admitted to St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital for her treatment. She went through more than dozens of Chemotherapy sessions.

She went through limb preservation surgery for her knee replacement. A titanium rod was placed in her left thigh bone.

She is now done with her physical therapy and other treatments.

The cancer survivor is working as a physician assistant at the very hospital where she was treated.

She is one of the four civilians who will be on board the spaceship in Earth’s orbit. She will be the Chief Medical Officer for the crew till her journey.

The mission aims to raise $200 m for St. Jude, and Isaac will be donating half of it.

Hayley Arceneaux suffered blood cancer at a young age.

Hayley Arceneaux has reached the age of 29 years. She was born on 4th December 1991 in Baton Rouge, Louisiana.

She was raised in St. Francisville, Louisiana, with her family.

When she was 10 years old, she was diagnosed with blood cancer.

And now, in her late 20s, she is going to make history by being the first person with prostheses to launch to space.

She has become the source of inspiration for people who are struggling with their own condition. Her journey to the spaceship makes people believe that anything is possible.
