Chris Pratt Controversy Explained, Chris Pratt Criticised For Praising Wifes healthy Baby F

April 2024 · 3 minute read

He talk a little too much.

People are calling Chris Pratt “vile and cringe-worthy” for praising his wife Katherine Schwarzenegger for trying to give him a “healthy daughter” when his son Jack with his ex-wife Anna Faris has had serious health problems over the years.

Thursday, someone tweeted, “I love Chris Pratt, but the “healthy daughter” part made me cringe.” “He might not have implied it as an insult, but that’s how it came across. “Can you imagine how his son will feel if he ever reads that?”

“Chris Pratt has a child with Anna Farris who was born with a cerebral hemorrhage and has some slight physical problems because of it,” said a more angry person. But he was sure to write “healthy daughter” here. Chris Pratt is a scumbag.

On Thursday, Pratt, who is 42 years old, wrote a birthday post for his wife that was six weeks early and included the controversial comment.

He wrote in the caption of a photo of him and Schwarzenegger, 31, “She’s given me an incredible life, a beautiful healthy daughter, and she chews so noisily that sometimes I have to put my earbuds in to drown it out, but that’s love!”

As was said, Pratt’s son Jack, who is now 9, was born early and had to have many surgeries because of it. He was born weighing only 3 pounds and 12 ounces.

“It was a big surprise when Jack was born two months early. And all of a sudden, your whole world changes,” Faris, who is 44 years old, said in 2018 on her podcast “Unqualified.” “Jack had several operations. He’s had a few surgeries for hernias, a few for his eyes, and a small heart problem.

At a March of Dimes Celebration of Babies event in 2014, Pratt also talked about his son’s early struggles.

“I made promises about what kind of dad I wanted to be at that moment, and I just prayed that he’d live long enough so I could keep them.” 

“Our Jack went from being a small, helpless baby to a strong, smart, happy, funny, handsome boy who loves monster trucks, “Daniel Tiger,” and, believe it or not, vegetables. His favorite foods are broccoli and cherry tomatoes.”

In 2018, when Jack was six years old, Pratt and Faris got a divorce. Then, in June 2019, the “Tomorrow War” star wedded Schwarzenegger. In August 2020, they had their first child, a baby girl named Lyla Maria.  Page Six asked Pratt and Faris’s reps for a comment, but they didn’t get back to us right away.
