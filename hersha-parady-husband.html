Hersha Parady Husband: Was Hersha Parady Married?: Hersha Parady, officially known as Betty Sandhoff was an American actress born on May 25, 1945, in Berea, Ohio, United States.

She developed a passion for acting at a younger age and began acting locally (in Berea, Ohio) at age 14 in Cleveland area theatrical productions.

Parady’s first big break was in theatre where she landed a role as Stella in a touring production of A Streetcar Named Desire.

She went ahead to star in a couple of films but became a household name following her role as Alice Garvey in Little House on the Prairie.

She played Alice Garvey in Little House on the Prairie from 1977 until 1980 when her character was killed in a fire accidentally set by Albert Ingalls.

Her character, Alice Garvey perished in a fire after running into a burning building to try to save a blind boy and baby.

Apart from her role in Little House On The Prairie, she guest appeared in The Waltons, and also played Principal Dimly in the 90s sitcom Kenan & Kel.

Her other film credits include; Hyper Sapien: People from Another Star, Bearcats, Mannix, The The Waltons, The Break, Raw Courage, and The Phoenix, among others.

Sadly the renowned actress has died. Hersha Parady passed away at 78 on Wednesday, April 23 2023 in Norfolk, Virginia.

Her son, Jonathan Peverall, confirmed her death on a GoFundMe page set up to aid with the cost of growing medical expenses and said she died at his home in Virginia, US.

The cause of death has not yet been made public but before the actress died, she had suffered from meningioma, a tumor in the membranes around the brain and spinal cord.
