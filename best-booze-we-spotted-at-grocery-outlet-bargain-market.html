Best Booze Bargains at Grocery Outlet Bargain Market

August 2024 · 3 minute read

If you’re in the market for booze, there’s a lot to love at Grocery Outlet Bargain Market. I’d split what I found on a recent visit into two categories: insane deals and Sporked-recommended products. Unfortunately, I couldn’t find many things in the store that fit both categories, but that’s okay! Whether you’re hunting for a good deal on something new or you want to grab something we loved in our taste tests, here’s what you should keep an eye out for at Grocery Outlet Bargain Market.

Heir Apparent Russian River Valley Pinot Noir

Do I know anything about wine? Unfortunately not. Can I read a price tag? Absolutely! This $90 wine is on sale for $15 at Grocery Outlet Bargain Market, so who among us can resist a deal like that? Pick up a couple bottles and store them away for when you want to truly impress a guest.

19 Crimes Snoop Dogg Cali Red Blend

Truthfully, my favorite reason to shop at Grocery Outlet Bargain Market is you can buy up gimmicky things, like celebrity-branded wines, and it turns out Snoop Dogg is there for us. I spotted 19 Crimes red wine on sale for just $5 (it’s usually $15), so you’re saving money and you get to try out the wine Snoop Dogg was willing to put his name on.

Elysian Space Dust IPA

Hops enthusiasts who shop at Grocery Outlet Bargain Market (a small but still mighty cross section, I’m sure) rejoice! You can pick up a six pack of Elysian Space Dust IPA on your next trip in. If you’re on the fence about IPAs, Sporked editor-in-chief Justine Sterling called this one “extremely drinkable,” so even if you’re a skeptic, this one is worth a shot.

Golden Road Mango Cart

If you’re looking for something more mellow than a powerful IPA, you should reach for Golden Road’s Mango Cart. This beer has a vibrant, tropical mango flavor that’s perf for an afternoon on the beach (or on the couch, dealer’s choice).

Pomp & Whimsy Gin Liqueur

We haven’t tackled spirits here at Sporked (although that sounds like a fun day at the office), but we can still appreciate a good deal. This gin liqueur was on sale for $10 (compared to the “elsewhere” price of $32), and that’s just too juicy to pass up. Now you just need some mixers, but he have ideas.

Wildcard Hard Original Lemonade

I nearly toppled over when I saw this four pack of hard lemonade on sale for $3 at Grocery Outlet Bargain Market. I couldn’t sneeze in a bar in Los Angeles for less than $3. Drop everything you’re doing and get to the nearest Grocery Outlet Bargain Market and see if they have these in stock!

Read our ranking of the best hard lemonade

Loverboy Sparkling Hard Teas Variety Pack

Lily Cousins thinks you should ditch hard seltzer for this drink. I could leave it at that, but okay, I won’t be that lazy. Loverboy Half & Half (which is included in this variety pack) was one of the Sporked crew’s favorite hard teas. Managing editor Gwynedd Stuart liked that it actually tastes like tea and has a little bit of fizz. And, hey, you can save $3 if you buy it at GOBM!

Read the full ranking of the best hard tea

Jiant Taco Tuesday

In a sea of hard kombuchas, choose the best of the best according to Gwynedd. This hard ‘booch is flavored with jalapeño and pineapple, so it’s a little sweet, a little savory, and a lot delicious. Pick up some ingredients for a true taco Tuesday to go along with a sixer of these bad boys!
