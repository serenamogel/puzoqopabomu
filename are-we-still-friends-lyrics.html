About

Tyler, The Creator is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, and music video director. He was born on March 6, 1991, in Ladera Heights, California. Tyler first gained recognition as the leader of the hip-hop collective Odd Future, which he formed with several high school friends. He released his debut album “Goblin” in 2011, which received mixed reviews but was praised for its dark and introspective lyrics.

Since then, Tyler has released several successful albums, including “Flower Boy” (2017), which received critical acclaim and earned him a Grammy nomination for Best Rap Album. He won the award for Best Rap Album at the 2020 Grammy Awards for his fifth studio album “Igor”.

In addition to his music career, Tyler has also ventured into fashion, launching his clothing brand Golf Wang in 2011. He has also created a television show called “The Jellies!” and has directed several music videos, including those for songs by Frank Ocean, A$AP Rocky, and Earl Sweatshirt.
