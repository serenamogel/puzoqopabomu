Shop Taylor Swift's Black Turtleneck 2024

July 2024 · 2 minute read

If there is one winter staple piece you need in your wardrobe, it’s a classic black turtleneck. Just ask Taylor Swift — who has been known to rock a turtleneck time and again. The singer was spotted at a Kansas City Chiefs Game earlier this month wearing a long-sleeved shirt with a fluffy red coat (so holiday-coded!). Swift was also *casually* chosen as Time’s Person of The Year, where she appeared on the cover pictured in a black turtle-necked bodysuit, black sheer tights, and her iconic bright red lip. Oh, and her cat was thrown over her shoulders, so do what you will with that information.

You can replicate Taylor’s style with Lululemon’s Wundermost Ultra-Soft Nulu Turtleneck Bodysuit in black for $88. The suit also comes in the colors riverstone, storm teal, bone (AKA white), and espresso, with sizes ranging from extra small to extra large. The espresso is already sold out in a small, so act fast if you're also looking to add a brown-inspired bodysuit to your closet. According to the product description, the bodysuit fabric is soft and made for layering (so you can even throw on a red coat as well if you're totally on board with emulating Swift’s style). In our opinion, a fashion piece that can be paired with anything and is going to be worn a lot is worth the price.

If you aren’t a layering type of person and are searching for a thicker sweater to wear as is, then this highly-rated black Abercrombie & Fitch Turtleneck Sweater Bodysuit may be the right choice. Perfect with the holidays right around the corner, whether you are snagging the turtleneck as a gift or planning to wear it at your upcoming Christmas party! Maybe you can even bring your cat as an adorable accessory. Maybe.
