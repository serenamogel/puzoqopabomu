Thomas Barbusca Career

Thomas Barbusca has carved a niche for himself as a young actor to watch, with a string of impressive roles in TV and film.

One of the most notable chapters in Barbusca’s career began with his portrayal of Chip Pemberton in the hit television sitcom, “The Mick.” Premiering in 2016, the show introduced Barbusca to a wider audience and established him as a rising star.

Barbusca’s big-screen debut came with the film “Middle School: The Worst Years of My Life”, where he played the role of Leo.

In addition to “The Mick” and “Wet Hot American Summer: First Day of Camp,” where he played the camp bully kid Drew, Barbusca’s television career expanded to include a role in the fifth season of “American Horror Story.”

These diverse roles not only showcased his acting range but also hinted at the potential for an enduring career in the industry.
