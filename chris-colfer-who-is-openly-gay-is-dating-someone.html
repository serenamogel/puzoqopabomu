Chris Colfer, Who Is Openly Gay, Is Dating Someone

September 2024 · 3 minute read

After playing Kurt Hummel on the TV singing program Glee, American actor Christopher Paul, better known by his stage as Chris Colfer, shot into popularity. Chris has gained a large following of admirers who want to date the great actor in real life due to his superb acting abilities and captivating appearance.

Chris, on the other hand, is happily dating a man who works in the same field as him. The California native from Clovis was also seen with his companion at several gatherings and events.

Is Chris Colfer Single? Relationship, Boyfriend

Chris Colfer, who was formerly linked to Darren Criss and Heather Morris, is an openly gay man who dates Will Sherrod and they have a wonderful relationship. Despite the fact that the media is aware of the couple’s dating life, neither Chris nor Will has revealed the details of how their relationship began.

Chris and his actor-boyfriend first sparked dating rumors after they attended the Screen Actors Guild Awards together in January 2013. The two took a flight to Paris in the same year to support Chris’s movie, Struck By Lightning.

Furthermore, the couple was photographed wearing matching outfits while enjoying the Fourth of July holiday at Mendocino Farms in West Hollywood. Even at Elton’s Oscar Party, they showed up and sat at a table with Ashley Fink, Chris’s best friend, and ex-Glee castmate. Throughout the festival, the two were spotted walking around the stages of Coachella together.

Chris has made his affection evident not only at events and other times but also on social media. On October 28, 2013, he posted photos of himself, his boyfriend, and their fluffy cat in Halloween costumes on Instagram. Numerous incidents show that the couple has been spending valuable time together.

Even though it means shattering the hearts of many Chris fans who aspire to be his girlfriend, let’s hope the couple’s love last forever and they soon get married to advance their relationship to a new level.

Author Chris Colfer

Well, Chris’s mother deserves praise for motivating him growing up!
